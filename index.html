Paperview

A newspaper like multicolumn reader mode for long form articles on the web.

Drag this link to your bookmarks bar to install:

Paperview

Now find a web page with a long article. Click the Paperview bookmarklet to reformat the page free of distractions. The article will be formatted in narrow columns like a newspaper.

You can navigate to the next page of the article by pressing the spacebar. To go back, press shift+space. Or, press left/right arrow keys.

Visit the Paperview project page for more information.
